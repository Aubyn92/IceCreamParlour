Advice for new staff

There's free bread at "Sporties" staff bar/cafe some days of the week. Just check in! As well as cheaper alcohol than at IGA liquor store ;)

Book exchange available at the staff library, and some free kitchen/house items in the cabinet for you to take if you need!

The free gym is just down the road from Cocos (our apartment). Literally less than a minute walk...so no excuses then! :)

Resort Link is a taxi type of service that usually costs $8 but is free at night from 11:30pm-1:45am, and will take you anywhere you need around the island.

Barge boat can be taken free of charge to Shute Harbour every day, except Sunday. Runs twice a day around 9-10am and again in the afternoon.

You can take the Whitsundays cruise ferry to Airlie Beach for $60 return if you don't fancy taking the free barge. Timetable can be found online or at work.

Marina Daze is held every Sunday evening from around 6pm onwards, down at the marina. There's music, bring your snacks etc!

"Fresh meat Wednesdays" is held on ....Wednesdays. It's at Sporties bar, late, and a great place to meet new people that have just arrived on the island.

Dent yoga on Sunday mornings every now and then. Just check the activities board at Sporties. Around 6am and around $30. Includes transport to Dent Island and a healthy buffet breakfast.

Airlie Beach is a resort town and the main street is a hub for shopping! There's a swimming lagoon and a market selling clothes, jewellery and crafts every Saturday.

Send all your personal mail to your work address! There's no mailbox for your apartment.
